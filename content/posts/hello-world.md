I'm a data engineer who spends most of his time working with Python, SQL, and cloud infrastructure. Over the past year, I've been deep in algorithmic trading, AI agent development, and building tools that blur the line between data engineering and software engineering.

This blog is where I'll share what I learn along the way — the things that worked, the things that didn't, and the mental models that made the difference.

Topics you'll find here:

- **Data engineering** — pipelines, DuckDB, parquet, time series data at scale
- **Software engineering** — Python patterns, CLI tools, system design
- **AI and agent engineering** — building with LLMs, Claude Code, autonomous agents
- **Quantitative finance** — mean reversion, backtesting, signal processing

If any of that sounds interesting, stick around.
